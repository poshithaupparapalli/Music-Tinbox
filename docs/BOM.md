# Bill of Materials (BOM)

> Fill this in as you research parts. Check DigiKey, Mouser, and your makerspace.
> Prices change — look them up yourself rather than trusting old numbers.

## How to Use This

1. Open Sage's schematic in KiCad
2. For each component, find the value and footprint
3. Search for it on DigiKey (digikey.com) or Mouser (mouser.com)
4. Write down the part number and price
5. Check if your makerspace has any of these — could save you $$$
